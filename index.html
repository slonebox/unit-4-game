Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your random score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is unknown to you until click on it.

Each time the game starts, the random number and crystal values will change.

Good luck!
